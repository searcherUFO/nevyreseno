Maison

Néoclassique

Une Maison dédiée à l'élégance. Ancrée dans la tradition, touchée par l'invention. Chaque produit incarne la splendeur de Versailles, la grâce et l'art du Louvre, et l'essence magique de Paris.

Le Néoclassicisme, avec son dévouement à la raison et à la beauté, définit l'esprit de la Maison — où le raffinement est un geste sacré vers l'excellence. La Maison suit le principe célèbre de la « Noblesse oblige ». Elle représente la responsabilité, le soin et l'intégrité — où le heritage meets modern ecological values.

This philosophy guides every creation: honoring craftsmanship, respecting the origin of each element, and offering more than a product — an invitation to experience history through each piece and transform it into lasting impressions. From this heritage rises the House's motto: — It's time for magic.

The House archetype is The Magician — a creator, a transformer, a guardian of wonder. This archetype holds the power to elevate the ordinary, transforming the present into an experience imbued with timeless elegance and the craftsmanship of a bygone era, inviting us to savor the perfect harmony of past and present.

A magician brand is an alchimist of time and experience, weaving with finesse the refined grace of the Belle Époque, the delicate complexity of Baroque and Rococo with the requirements of the contemporary world. Maison du Lys est.Paris sublimes its creations into history-steeped experiences, where the modern product dialogues with the richness of the visual and sensory traditions of the sublime heritage we admire in France, in Paris.

Neoclassicism is both an artistic movement and a stylistic period that emerged around 1750 in the Europe of the Enlightenment, contemporary with and following the vogue of the Grand Tour. Wikipedia

Neoclassical Refers to the Assimilation of Classical Cultural Norms In the Modern World.


Maison du Lys contributes to the assimilation of classical cultural norms and forms in the modern world, regarding traditions, aesthetics, culture and art.

By creating products with visual effects that evoke a feeling of elegance and romanticism, as well as a wonderful feeling of touching the history and beauty of France. - To convey the charm of France and its unique style, which leaves no one indifferent.

Étymologie de « classicisme »

From the French word classique, derived with the suffix -isme. Appeared in 1817 with the meaning of 'character of literary works referring to ancient art' and in 1875 with the meaning of 'character of what is harmonious, balanced'.

Étymologie de « néo-classicisme »

Derived from classicism, with the prefix neo-.

Wiktionnaire

The term comes from the Latin classicus which means "first class",

the idea of quality and excellence was retained in French.

Brand Licensing Program

Présentation

Licenses are offered to operate mini-boutiques, large boutiques, corners, kiosks and mobile sales carts. Each format respects precise interior and exterior design standards, guaranteeing visual and material consistency while allowing for local adaptations according to the country, city or commercial environment.

What we provide

The right to use the brand's visual identity and logo. Access to approved product collections. Complete structures (boutique, kiosk or mobile cart) provided by the brand. Customization adapted to the region and location. Complete guidelines on design and presentation. Assistance during setup and opening. Comprehensive training covering installation, implementation, sales, marketing and branding, adapted to your country, city and site.

License formats

Boutiques — independent retail spaces

Mini-boutiques / Corners — spaces integrated within partner stores

Kiosks / Mobile sales carts — modular units for public spaces, events or high-traffic areas

We have a contract with a cart manufacturer in France, the USA and internationally. For more information and to obtain a quote, please contact us.

Terms and fees

One-time license fee, depending on location, format and size. Annual fees or royalties, calculated based on sales and the format of the point of sale. All terms are defined and validated before approval.

Process

- Submit a request
- Define format and location
- Review and approval
- Receipt of structure and design kit
- Opening according to brand guidelines

Principles

Each licensed space embodies the brand values — refined materials, balanced proportions and functional elegance. Regional adaptations are possible while respecting the integrity and consistency of the brand.
